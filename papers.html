A Reflection on Masculinity in Contemporary Urban Egypt

This was written for a class on Women in the Middle East. The articles referenced in the paper are the second chapter of Farha Ghannam’s book Live and Die Like a Man: Gender Dynamics in Urban Egypt and the first chapter of Hannan Hamad’s book Industrial Sexuality: Gender, Urbanization, and Social Transformation in Egypt. Within this paper, I examine how social class affects gender identity and expression thereof.
