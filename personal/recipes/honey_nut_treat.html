Honey Nut Treat

1/4 c pecans, chopped

1/4 c walnuts, chopped

1/4 c almonds, chopped

1/4 c rolled oats

2 c brown sugar

1/2 c water

1/2 c butter, melted

1 c honey

1 tsp salt

2 tsp cinnamon

2 tsp nutmeg


  1. In a large/medium pot, combine the sugar, honey, water, salt, and butter. Cook on medium-high heat until boiling, stirring continually to avoid burning, for about 10 mins. DO NOT OVERCOOK.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine your nuts, oats, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Pour the caramel in and stir well with a large spoon.
  3. While the mixture is still warm, shape into medium-sized balls and attach them together with a skewer, 3 balls per skewer.
  4. Place your skewers on a tray lined with baking paper, and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
